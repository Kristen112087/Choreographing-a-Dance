Song Choice

If you want to Choreograph your own line dance, the first step is to pick your music!

It's all about what appeals to you, this is your dance, pick whatever you want!

  1. Open Spotify
  2. Search title or genre you like
  3. pick one that has a good beat that you like

Next step is to count out your dance

You want to count the number of beats, so you can have the correct number of steps in your dance.

  1. Get pencil and paper
  2. Start song
  3. Count each 8 count as 1 mark on your paper
  4. When there is a break in the song or the chorus starts, start a new line
  5. When the song is done, you now have the number of counts in the song

Creating and Submitting your Stepsheet

  1. Write down each step you take
  2. Go over it many times to make sure each step is correct
  3. Submit your line dance to Copperknob
  4. Record a video of you dancing your dance and upload to Copperknob
